about Uranus

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and is classified as an ice giant. It rotates on its side, causing extreme seasons and has a blue-green color due to methane.

data about Uranus

Orbital Period: 84 Earth years
Rotation Period: 17.2 hours
Diameter: 50,724 km
Mass: 8.68 × 10²⁵ kg
Surface Gravity: 8.69 m/s²
Average Temperature: -197°C
Moons: 27
Atmosphere: Hydrogen, Helium, Methane
