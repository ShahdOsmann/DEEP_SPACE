about Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and the second-largest planet in the Solar System. It is famous for its spectacular ring system and is a gas giant composed mainly of hydrogen and helium.

data about Saturn

Orbital Period: 29.5 Earth years
Rotation Period: 10.7 hours
Diameter: 120,536 km
Mass: 5.68 × 10²⁶ kg
Surface Gravity: 10.44 m/s²
Average Temperature: -140°C
Moons: 83+
Atmosphere: Hydrogen, Helium, Methane
