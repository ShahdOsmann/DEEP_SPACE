About Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to support life. It has a diameter of about 12,742 km and is the largest terrestrial planet in the Solar System. Earth has a diverse atmosphere composed mainly of nitrogen and oxygen, which protects life from harmful solar radiation and helps regulate temperature. Its surface is about 71% water, with vast oceans, and 29% land. Earth rotates every 24 hours and completes one orbit around the Sun in 365.25 days. The Moon influences tides and stabilizes Earth's axial tilt. Earth’s magnetic field protects life from solar winds and cosmic radiation.
