about Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is similar in size and structure to Earth, often called Earth's twin. It has a thick toxic atmosphere causing extreme greenhouse effect and is the hottest planet.

data about Venus

Orbital Period: 225 Earth days
Rotation Period: 243 Earth days (retrograde)
Diameter: 12,104 km
Mass: 4.87 × 10²⁴ kg
Surface Gravity: 8.87 m/s²
Average Temperature: 465°C
Moons: None
Atmosphere: Thick CO₂, clouds of sulfuric acid
