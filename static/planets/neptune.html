about Neptune

Neptune is the eighth and farthest planet from the Sun in the Solar System. It is classified as an ice giant due to its cold atmosphere and composition of hydrogen, helium, and ices like water, ammonia, and methane.

data about Neptune

Orbital Period: 164.8 Earth years
Rotation Period: 16.1 hours
Diameter: 49,244 km
Mass: 1.02 × 10²⁶ kg
Surface Gravity: 11.15 m/s²
Average Temperature: -200°C
Moons: 14
Atmosphere: Hydrogen, Helium, Methane
